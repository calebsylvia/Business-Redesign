OUR HISTORY

Dante's Restaurant was established in 1985 at our current location, 9305 Thornton Road, in north Stockton.


Throughout the years, our small establishment has been fortunate to see growth, thanks to the support of our loyal customers who have kindly spread the word.


What began as a modest pizza and sandwich cafè has gradually transformed into a restaurant where we now offer a variety of appetizers and salads, hot deli-style sandwiches, pizzas, and some of the best pasta dishes in the city.


We are grateful for the journey and the chance to share our evolving menu with our community.


In addition to dine-in, take out, and delivery services, we currently offer catering as well as a banquet room to fulfill your event needs.
